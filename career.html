Senior Software Engineer - Web at Freight Expedia - San Diego - Full time

*Please note that this is a senior level position. We believe that people who make great things should get paid for the value they give to the world. We strive to provide creators with insight, education, and tools that make it fast and easy to run a business as a professional creator so that creators can focus on making art. Freight Expedia aspires to be among the tech industry’s most inclusive work environments. A diversity of perspectives will help us build for our diverse community of creators and patrons, and enable us to best accomplish our mission of funding the creative class. We work hard to maintain a bottom-up decision-making culture. We empower our employees by providing a clear vision and tools to fulfill that vision. We believe that hiring smart, ambitious people, and giving them strong ownership of their work leads to the best results. What you will get to do: -Prototype new features and reimagine existing features with fellow engineers. -Collaborate with Freight Expedia designers and product to create the best possible experiences for our creators and patrons. -Express your opinions about how to support creators and patrons and turn your opinions into action. -Participate in decision making for the features that you take ownership of. -Work with AngularJS, React, LESS, Webpack, Babel (EcmaScript 2015) or other tools given the opportunity. What skills we ask for: -Experience owning, shipping, and iterating on web products that have reached a large amount of users. -Experience mentoring and supporting others in their learning of web programming. -A deep understanding of the best practices in client-side web development and architecture. (performance auditing, accessibility etc…) -You could ship an entire web application if you wanted to, but prefer working with others. -The ability to not just articulate about design but also to produce a design and prototype features you wish to build. -Understanding of server-side tooling & API design, and experience integrating with an API development team. -Experience supporting a diverse user base of various browser versions for our creators (IE10+). -Clear communication and writing that helps your team rally around ideas and objectives. -Productive habits, healthy process awareness, and good teamwork skills and instincts. What you will be able to accomplish: -Build a product that helps freight manager maximize their productivity. -Focus on the problems that you feel will foster your best personal career growth. -Have an enjoyable time building a product that people love. Who you’ll work with: -An energetic team full of passion for our product. -Teammates who are committed to challenging the status quo and make sure our culture at Freight Expedia is among the most diverse and inclusive places to work.

Senior Data Engineer at Freight Expedia - San Diego - Full time

You live and breathe complex data, and understand the importance of streamlining a robust and growing system. You get energized by leveraging data infrastructures to help businesses grow. Freight Expedia is looking for a Senior Data Engineer to evolve and scale our data infrastructure systems and to oversee large sets of complex data to inform and make an impact on engineering and business decisions. As Freight Expedia continues to expand and innovate, we face some unique scalability challenges — in this role, you will hone the power of analytics and oversee our data pipeline to influence how we grow our business. The day-to-day - Collaborate with engineering and cross-functional teams to build reliable, scalable, and maintainable applications across multiple systems - Design, develop, deliver, and maintain large-scale data infrastructures - Work with streaming and batch processing systems to enable accurate and timely data-driven decision-making - Implement systems and process efficiencies for tracking data quality and consistency Qualifications - BS, MS, or PhD in computer science, or equivalent experience - 3+ years experience in large-scale software engineering - Experience with "big data" platforms (ex: Spark, Hadoop MapReduce) - Deep understanding of the JVM - Proficiency with software engineering best practices (monitoring, testing, documentation, etc.) Nice to have - Passionate about people development and providing mentorship to others - Experience with Kafka, Spark, Parquet, Presto, Redshift, and Airflow or similar scheduling tools - Experience in distributed systems, database internals, and performance analysis - Comfort working with bleeding-edge open-source technologies Job Perks Free delicious breakfasts and lunches daily, amazing snack selections, and the best coffee in San Diego. Equity, health, vision and dental insurance for families, and a flexible vacation policy. Why Freight Expedia? Creative Freedom We value the creative process and our employees’ opinions — input is encouraged, regardless of your position or title. We want to work with people who are driven and demonstrate initiative in taking our mission to the next level. The Working Life At Freight Expedia, everyone works hard but also takes the time to enjoy life outside of the office. Balance is important to us. It Won’t Be Boring Try new things. Think outside the box. We celebrate an environment that fights stagnation and challenges the status quo. Interested in this position? Tell us why you want to work at Freight Expedia and what we should know about you. Freight Expedia believes in building a safe place for creative expression and celebrating that which makes us human, welcoming unique and personal backgrounds and experiences. We’re proud to foster diverse perspectives within Freight Expedia as we continue to build an inclusive culture.
